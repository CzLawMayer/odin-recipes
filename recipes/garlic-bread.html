Garlic Bread Recipe

Home

Description

Garlic bread is the perfect sidekick to any Italian dish, offering a buttery, garlicky crunch that’s hard to resist. It’s quick to prepare, yet brings big flavor with every bite.

Toasty on the outside and soft on the inside, this recipe uses simple ingredients to create a crave-worthy classic. It’s ideal for soaking up sauces or serving as a starter that sets the tone for a great meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette or loaf of Italian bread
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3–4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley (or 1 tbsp dried)
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ cup shredded mozzarella or Parmesan (optional, for cheesy garlic bread)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix butter, garlic, parsley, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Slice bread lengthwise and spread butter mixture evenly.
  4. (Optional) Add shredded cheese on top.
  5. Bake for 10–12 mins, then broil for 1–2 mins for crispiness.
  6. Slice and serve warm.
