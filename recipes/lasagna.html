Lasagna Recipe

Home

Description

Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food, layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and perfectly baked pasta sheets. It’s a hearty, satisfying dish that brings people together around the table, whether for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion.

This classic Italian favorite balances savory flavors, gooey cheese, and just the right amount of herbs. Once you master the layering technique, it becomes a go-to recipe you’ll want to make again and again.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles (cooked)
  • 1 lb ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara or pasta sauce
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for sautéing

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, sauté onion and garlic in olive oil, add ground beef, cook until browned.
  4. Stir in pasta sauce, season with salt, pepper, oregano, and basil. Simmer for 10 mins.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, and a bit of Parmesan.
  6. In a baking dish, layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mix, mozzarella—repeat layers.
  7. Top with remaining cheese and bake for 25–30 mins until bubbly and golden.
  8. Let it rest for 10 mins before serving.
