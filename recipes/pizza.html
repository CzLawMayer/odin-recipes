Pizza Recipe

Home

Description

Homemade pizza is a fun and delicious way to get creative in the kitchen. Whether you like it loaded with toppings or simple with just cheese and sauce, pizza offers endless customization and crowd-pleasing results.

With a crispy crust, flavorful tomato base, and melty cheese, every bite is a mix of textures and tastes. This recipe gives you the freedom to make it your own while sticking to classic methods that never go out of style.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 ¼ tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp salt

For the dough:

  • ½ cup pizza sauce or marinara
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of choice (pepperoni, bell peppers, mushrooms, olives, etc.)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp garlic powder (optional)
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. In warm water, dissolve yeast and sugar. Let it sit for 5–10 mins until foamy.
  2. Mix in flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead into a dough.
  3. Cover and let rise for 1 hour or until doubled.
  4. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  5. Roll out dough, place on a baking tray or pizza stone.
  6. Spread sauce, add cheese and toppings.
  7. Bake for 12–15 mins or until crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  8. Cool slightly, slice, and enjoy!
